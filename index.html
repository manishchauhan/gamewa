Welcome to "Chrono Aviator: Professor Ashwatthama"!

You are Professor Ashwatthama, a genius mathematician and inventor stuck in World War 1 during 1912. A strange event related to 'Time Tampering' puts you in a place called the Time Well, surrounded by historical chaos. But don't worry, you're not alone. You've built a special airplane using parts from broken planes.

Here's how to play:
  1. Fly Your Plane: Scroll the screen or move the mouse to steer the airplane.
  2. Collect Stars: Look for the stars. These stars are your cosmic fuel. Collect them as you fly by touching them.
  3. Power Up: The more stars you collect, the more powerful your plane becomes. This lets you fly faster and escape the Time Well's grasp.
  4. BEWARE: Beware of the flying boulders.

Remember, the stars are your key to success. Collect them to make your airplane stronger and achieve your mission of escaping the Time Well and setting things right in history.

Enjoy your celestial adventure!
